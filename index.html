FluidSim

An iOS app wrapping a realtime, particle-based fluid simulation, written in C++. Rendering is done in a custom-written GLSL post-processing shader, with extensive configurability via a settings page.

Features:
  • Smoothed-particle hydrodynamics incompressible fluid simulation
  • Beautiful water rendering with top shimmer, speed effects, and depth scattering
  • Customizable gravity, viscosity, color, and other visual effects
  • Adding, removing, and flinging of fluid via touch interaction
